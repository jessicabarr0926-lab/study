Core concept

Conclusion vs. Evidence

Because...For example...So the author believes...

Animated script

Do not start by asking whether the argument is good. Start by asking which idea depends on the other ideas. The dependent claim is the conclusion; the supporting ideas are evidence.

Key takeaways

  • Conclusion indicators help, but sentence function matters more.
  • Evidence usually answers why the author believes the conclusion.
  • Counterpoints and concessions can appear before the real claim.

Drill prompt

For the next five LR stimuli, write only two labels: conclusion and evidence. Speed comes after accuracy.
