Question type

Role Questions: Name the Job

ClaimSupportWhat job does this sentence do?

Animated script

Role questions ask you to zoom in on one sentence without losing the whole argument. Label the argument first, then describe the highlighted sentence's job in that structure.

Key takeaways

  • Ask whether the sentence supports, is supported, challenges, concedes, or explains.
  • Do not confuse content with function.
  • Use nearby transition words to see how the sentence connects.

Journal prompt

When you miss a role question, write the sentence job you chose and the job it actually played.
