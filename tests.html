Argumentative Writing

Practice the writing sample.

Lesson

  1. Read both options and pick a clear side.
  2. Use two criteria: why your side wins and why the other side is weaker.
  3. Write in four blocks: position, reason one, reason two, concession.
  4. Leave two minutes to clean up transitions.

Practice prompt

A city must choose between funding a late-night bus route or expanding a legal-aid clinic. The city values broad access, measurable impact, and long-term public benefit. Choose one option and defend it.
